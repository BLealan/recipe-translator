Welcome to our Recipe Translator

Explore Culinary Delights

Welcome to Recipe Translator! This website allows you to search for recipes and translates the recipe names, ingredients, and instructions into different languages. Use the search bar to find your favorite recipes and explore the world of cuisine.

How to Use:

1. Enter the name of the meal in the search bar.

2. Click the "Search" button to find recipes.

3. Explore the displayed recipes, including images, ingredients, and instructions.

4. Use the language dropdown to select your preferred language.

5. Click "Translate Ingredients" or "Translate Instructions" to see translations.
